The Beta Program

Bhalo!Bangla is being created with the goal of creating a new online Bengali Learning Program for the English-speaking Bengali community. This program enables students to maintian a connection with family/nation and strengthen an Islamic identity.

The program is still in beta. What does that mean? Well, the technology, curriculum, teachers, management, and infastructure are still in development. But it also means you'll be amoungst the first to experience the program and you'll be a pioneer in spreading Bengali learning worldwide.

The goal of the beta program particularly is understanding the needs of the community. How shold we pivot our work? How can we best serve Bengalis across the world?

Program Structure

  • One, two, or three hours of live-online Bengali tutoring at $8/hour
  • Supplementary resources that are still in development like vocab lists, videos, games
  • One Paragraph of feedback after each lesson
  • Additional Opportunities to provide feedback including surveys, and interviews
