Beta Invite

Bhalo!Bangla is a new online Bengali Learning Program for the English-speaking Bengali community. This program enables students to maintian a connection with family/nation and thus retain an Islamic identity.

The program is still in beta. Thus, the technology, curriculum, teachers, management, and infastructure are still in development.

The goal of the beta program particularly is understanding the needs of the community. How can we best serve Bengalis across the world?

Program Structure

  • One, two, or three hours of live-online Bengali tutoring at $8/hour
  • Supplementary resources that are still in development like vocab lists, videos, games
  • One Paragraph of feedback after each lesson
  • Opportunities to provide feedback including surveys, and interviews
